SIP Calculator

Understanding Your SIP Investment

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a disciplined way of investing, where you invest a fixed amount of money at regular intervals. This calculator helps you estimate the future value of your monthly SIP investments.

How to Use the Calculator

  1. Monthly Investment: Enter the amount you plan to invest each month.
  2. Expected Return Rate: Enter the annual rate of return you expect from your investment.
  3. Time Period: Enter the total duration in years for which you plan to invest.
