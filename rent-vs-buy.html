Understanding the Rent vs. Buy Decision

Deciding whether to rent or buy a home is one of the biggest financial decisions you'll make. This calculator is designed to help you compare the long-term financial implications of both options.

By inputting details about the property you're considering buying and what you would pay in rent for a similar home, this tool estimates the total net cost of each path over your loan's tenure. It accounts for your down payment, loan payments, taxes, maintenance, and the potential for your property's value to grow over time.

How to Use the Calculator

  1. Renting Details: Enter your current or expected monthly rent.
  2. Buying Details: Fill in all the fields related to the property you're considering. Be as accurate as possible with rates and percentages.
  3. Compare: Click the "Compare" button to see a summary and a chart visualizing the total costs. The "Net Cost of Buying" subtracts the future value of your home from your total expenses to show your true financial position.
